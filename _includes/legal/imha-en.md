**Valiente Technologies Bilişim San. ve Tic. Ltd. Şti.** ("Valiente Technologies" or "Company") is committed to the security of personal data we process and to its destruction in accordance with applicable legislation. This Policy has been prepared pursuant to KVKK Law No. 6698 and related regulations.

#### 1. Storage Media

Your personal data is stored in the following secured environments:

- **Electronic Media:** Encrypted cloud storage systems, secure servers, local databases, and backup units.
- **Physical Media:** Company archive, locked filing cabinets, and physical documents.

#### 2. Grounds for Retention and Destruction

- **Retention:** Data is retained for periods required by law, for performance of contracts, as evidence in legal disputes, and for legitimate business interests.
- **Destruction:** When the processing purpose ceases, legal retention periods expire, or upon a valid request from the data subject, data is irreversibly destroyed.

#### 3. Retention Periods

| Data Category | Retention Period | Legal Grounds |
|---|---|---|
| Traffic (Log) Records | 2 Years | Law No. 5651 |
| Contact Form Records | 2 Years | Contract / Legitimate Interest |
| Customer / Partner Data | 10 Years from Contract End | Turkish Commercial Code / TCO |
| HR Data | 10 Years from Employment End | Labor Law / SSI |

#### 4. Destruction Methods

Data whose retention period has expired is destroyed using one of the following methods:

- **Deletion:** Making data inaccessible and non-reusable for authorized users.
- **Destruction:** Rendering data irrecoverable through technological methods (e.g., overwriting) or physical means (burning, shredding, etc.).
- **Anonymization:** Making data permanently unidentifiable, even if combined with other datasets.

#### 5. Periodic Destruction

Valiente Technologies has set a periodic destruction interval of **6 months**. Data whose retention period has expired is automatically destroyed each June and December.
