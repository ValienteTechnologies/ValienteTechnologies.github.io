#### 1. What is a Cookie?

Cookies are small text files saved to your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) through your browser when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize you, remember your preferences, and provide a faster and more secure experience.

#### 2. Types of Cookies Used and Their Purposes

- **Essential Cookies:** Technically required for the secure and proper operation of the website. Used for session management, bot protection (Cloudflare, etc.), and core site functionality. These cookies cannot be turned off.
- **Analytical Cookies:** Help us understand how visitors use our website. Anonymous data collected via **Google Analytics** is used to analyze traffic and improve site performance.
- **Functional Cookies:** Provide a more personalized experience by remembering preferences such as your language selection.

#### 3. Cookie Retention Periods

- **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies deleted from your device when you close your browser.
- **Persistent Cookies:** Stored on your device until you delete them or their expiration dates pass.

#### 4. Managing or Disabling Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may cause some Site features to not function properly.

- **Google Analytics Opt-out:** Use the browser add-on at [tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- **Browser Settings:** Manage cookies through the "Privacy and Security" settings in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge.
