**Valiente Technologies Bilişim San. ve Tic. Ltd. Şti.** ("Company") places the highest importance on the security and privacy of your personal data. In accordance with Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data ("KVKK"), we process your personal data as a "Data Controller" within the scope explained below and within the limits set by applicable legislation.

This Clarification Text has been prepared to provide detailed information about how we handle personal data of website visitors, individuals who complete contact forms, and business partners who use our services.

#### 1. Personal Data Processed and Purposes

- **Identity and Contact Information:** Your name, surname, email address, and phone number are processed for responding to your inquiries, conducting proposal and information processes for our cybersecurity services, customer relationship management, and strategic communication planning.
- **Transaction Security Information:** IP address, log records, device and browser information collected automatically during your visit; processed for managing cybersecurity operations, maintaining information security, auditing site functionality, and fulfilling legal obligations under Law No. 5651.
- **Marketing and Analysis Information:** Usage habits and preferences collected via cookies; processed for personalizing the user experience, measuring site performance, and improving service quality.

#### 2. Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal data may be transferred, within the purposes stated above and as permitted by law:

- To authorized public institutions (for legal compliance and judicial/administrative processes),
- To business partners, technical infrastructure suppliers, and cloud service providers (for operational processes and technical security),

within the framework of Articles 8 and 9 of the KVKK. All necessary administrative and technical security measures are taken during transfers.

#### 3. Method and Legal Grounds for Data Collection

Your personal data is collected fully or partially via automated means through contact forms, cookies, and server logs. Legal grounds for processing include:

- **Explicitly provided for in laws:** (Especially Law No. 5651 and related legislation).
- **Directly related to the establishment or performance of a contract:** (Meeting your service requests).
- **Fulfillment of the legal obligation of the data controller.**
- **Legitimate interest of the data controller:** (Cybersecurity, analytics, and protection of rights).
- **Explicit consent:** (For marketing-oriented cookies and where required by law).

#### 4. Your Rights as a Data Subject (KVKK Article 11)

Under KVKK, you have the right to:

1. Learn whether your personal data is being processed,
2. Request information if it is processed,
3. Learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used accordingly,
4. Know the third parties to whom data is transferred domestically or abroad,
5. Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data,
6. Request deletion or destruction under conditions stipulated by law,
7. Object to outcomes arising from automated analysis of your data,
8. Claim compensation for damages caused by unlawful processing.

You can submit your applications through the channels specified in the **Corporate Information and Contact** section.
