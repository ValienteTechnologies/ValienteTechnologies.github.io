# PERSONAL DATA RETENTION AND DESTRUCTION POLICY

As **Valiente Technologies Bilişim San. ve Tic. Ltd. Şti.** ("Valiente Technologies" or "Company"), we show great sensitivity to the security of the personal data we process and the processes of destruction in accordance with the legislation. This Policy has been prepared in accordance with KVKK and related regulations.

#### 1. Recording Media
Your personal data is stored in the following secured environments:
*   **Electronic Media:** Encrypted cloud storage systems, secure servers, local databases, and backup units.
*   **Physical Media:** Company archive, locked file cabinets, and physical documents.

#### 2. Reasons Requiring Retention and Destruction
*   **Retention:** Your data is stored for periods provided for in laws, for the performance of contract processes, to constitute evidence in legal disputes, and in line with legitimate interests.
*   **Destruction:** When the processing purpose ceases, legal retention periods expire, or upon the justified request of the data subject, data is irreversibly destroyed.

#### 3. Retention Periods (Summary)
| Data Category | Retention Period | Legal Grounds |
|---|---|---|
| Traffic (Log) Records | 2 Years | Law No. 5651 |
| Contact Form Records | 2 Years | Contract / Legitimate Interest |
| Customer/Business Partner Data | 10 Years from Contract Termination | TCC / TCO |
| HR Data | 10 Years from Employment Termination | Labor Law / SSI |

#### 4. Destruction Techniques
Data whose retention period has expired is destroyed by one of the following methods:
*   **Deletion:** Making data inaccessible and non-reusable for relevant users.
*   **Destruction:** Making data inaccessible technologically (through irreversible methods such as overwriting data) or physically (burning, shredding, etc.).
*   **Anonymization:** Making data unidentifiable even if matched with other data.

#### 5. Periodic Destruction
Valiente Technologies has determined the periodic destruction period as **6 months**. In June and December of each year, data whose retention period has expired is destroyed ex officio.

To obtain detailed information about our destruction processes, please contact us through the addresses in the **Corporate Information and Contact** section.
