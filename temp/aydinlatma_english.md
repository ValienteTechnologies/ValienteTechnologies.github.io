# CLARIFICATION TEXT ON PERSONAL DATA PROCESSING

As **Valiente Technologies Bilişim San. ve Tic. Ltd. Şti.** ("Company"), we place the highest importance on the security and privacy of your personal data. In accordance with the Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data ("KVKK"), we process your personal data as a "Data Controller" within the scope explained below and within the limits set by the legislation.

This Clarification Text has been prepared to provide detailed information about the processing of personal data of users visiting our website, individuals filling out contact forms, and our business partners benefiting from our services.

#### 1. Processed Personal Data and Purposes
Your personal data processed by our company may include the following depending on our processing purposes:
*   **Identity and Contact Information:** Your name, surname, email address, and phone number are processed for responding to your requests and inquiries, carrying out proposal and information processes regarding our cybersecurity services, customer relationship management, and strategic planning of communication activities.
*   **Transaction Security Information:** IP address, log records, device and browser information collected automatically during your visit to our website; processed for managing cybersecurity processes, ensuring information security at the highest level, auditing the technical functionality of the site, and fulfilling our legal obligations under Law No. 5651.
*   **Marketing and Analysis Information:** Usage habits and preferences collected through cookies; processed for personalizing the user experience, measuring site performance, and increasing our service quality.

#### 2. Transfer of Personal Data
Your personal data may be transferred, in line with the realization of the purposes stated above and to the extent permitted by legislation:
*   To authorized public institutions and organizations (for the purpose of fulfilling legal obligations and following judicial/administrative processes),
*   To our business partners, technical infrastructure suppliers, and cloud service providers (for the purpose of carrying out operational processes and ensuring technical security),
within the framework of the personal data processing conditions specified in Articles 8 and 9 of the KVKK. Our company takes all necessary administrative and technical security measures during data transfer.

#### 3. Method and Legal Grounds for Data Collection
Your personal data is collected fully or partially by automatic means through the contact forms on our website, cookies, and server logs. The legal grounds for our processing activities are:
*   **Explicitly provided for in laws:** (Especially Law No. 5651 and related legislation).
*   **Directly related to the establishment or performance of a contract:** (Meeting your service requests).
*   **Fulfillment of the legal obligation of the data controller.**
*   **Legitimate interest of the data controller:** (Cybersecurity, analytical development, and protection of rights).
*   **Explicit consent:** (For marketing-oriented cookies and in cases required by legislation).

#### 4. Your Rights as a Data Subject (Article 11 of KVKK)
In accordance with KVKK, you have the following rights regarding yourself by applying to our Company:
1. To learn whether personal data is processed,
2. To request information if processed,
3. To learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used in accordance with the purpose,
4. To know the third parties to whom it is transferred domestically or abroad,
5. To request correction if it is processed incompletely or incorrectly,
6. To request deletion or destruction within the framework of the conditions stipulated in the law,
7. To object to the emergence of a result against you by analyzing it exclusively through automated systems,
8. To request the compensation of the damage if you suffer damage due to unlawful processing.

To exercise these rights, you can submit your applications to us through the channels specified in the **Corporate Information and Contact** section.
