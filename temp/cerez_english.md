# COOKIE POLICY

As **Valiente Technologies Bilişim San. ve Tic. Ltd. Şti.** ("Valiente Technologies", "Company", or "we"), we use cookies to improve the user experience we provide through our website https://valientetechnologies.com ("Site") and to analyze visitor traffic.

#### 1. What is a Cookie?
Cookies are small text files saved to your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) through your browser when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize you, remember your preferences, and provide a faster/safer experience.

#### 2. Types of Cookies Used and Purposes
The cookies used on our site are divided into categories according to their intended use:
*   **Essential Cookies:** Technically necessary for the secure and proper operation of the website. They are used for session management, bot protection (Cloudflare, etc.), and basic site functionality. These cookies cannot be turned off.
*   **Analytical Cookies:** Help us understand how visitors use our website. Anonymous data collected through **Google Analytics** on our site is used to analyze traffic and increase site performance.
*   **Functional Cookies:** Provide a more personalized experience by remembering your preferences such as your language selection.

#### 3. Retention Period of Cookies
*   **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies deleted from your device when you close your browser.
*   **Persistent Cookies:** Cookies stored on your device until you delete them or until their expiration dates.

#### 4. How Can You Manage or Disable Cookies?
You can change your preferences regarding the use of cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may cause some features on the Site not to work properly.
*   **Google Analytics Opt-out:** You can use the browser add-on at [tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
*   **Browser Settings:** You can manage cookies through the "Privacy and Security" settings of popular browsers such as Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.

#### 5. Updates and Contact
Valiente Technologies may update this Cookie Policy at any time in line with legal regulations or changes in service processes. For detailed questions, please contact us through the addresses in the **Corporate Information and Contact** card.
