# PRIVACY POLICY

As **Valiente Technologies Bilişim San. ve Tic. Ltd. Şti.** ("Valiente Technologies", "Company", or "we"), protecting the privacy and security of our users visiting our website https://valientetechnologies.com ("Site") is our primary priority. This Privacy Policy comprehensively explains for what purposes your personal data is collected, how it is used, and your rights over your data.

#### 1. Data Collection and Processing Principles
In line with our expertise in cybersecurity, Valiente Technologies applies data privacy standards at the highest level. Your personal data is collected and processed solely for:
*   Providing and improving our services,
*   Maintaining healthy communication with you,
*   Ensuring technical security and operational efficiency.

#### 2. Types of Data Collected
*   **Contact Form Data:** Name, surname, email, phone, and message contents you transmit through forms on our website. This data is processed only to provide the service subject to your request.
*   **Analytical and Traffic Data:** Technical data such as IP address, geographic location, pages visited, browsing time, and referring URL collected during your visit to the Site. This data is used anonymously to analyze site traffic.
*   **Cookies:** Small text files saved to your device via your browser to improve the Site experience. See **Cookie Policy** for details.

#### 3. Data Security Standards
As a cybersecurity organization, we implement administrative and technical measures such as:
*   SSL-encrypted data transmission,
*   High-security server infrastructures and cloud systems,
*   Regular security audits and penetration tests,
*   Strict internal access controls and authorization procedures
for the security of your data.

#### 4. Sharing Data with Third Parties
Your personal data is not sold, rented, or transferred to third parties for commercial purposes in any way without your explicit consent. Your data may be shared only in the following cases:
*   **Legal Obligation:** In case of lawful request by judicial authorities or authorized administrative institutions.
*   **Operational Support:** With our business partners from whom we receive technical support for the hosting, security, or analytical measurements of our website, considering KVKK compliance processes.

#### 5. Third Party Links
The external links on the Site (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.) are subject to their own privacy policies. Valiente Technologies cannot be held responsible for the contents and privacy practices of third-party sites linked.

#### 6. Your Rights and Contact
To exercise your rights under KVKK such as obtaining information about the processing of your data, requesting correction, deletion, and objecting to processing activities, please contact us through the channels in the **Corporate Information and Contact** section.
